Welcome to NEXA 🎉

Everything you need to hit the ground running. Complete your Day 1 checklist, meet your team, and get fully set up in your first week.

✅ Day 1 Setup Checklist

  • ✓
    Connect to WiFi & sign into laptop
    Sign in via personal Microsoft account on first boot
  • ✓
    Set up Gmail — firstname@digitalnexa.com
    Send a test email to the Operations Manager before emailing any client
  • ✓
    Download Slack & sign in with NEXA credentials
    Join #general, #your-department, and #nexa-social on Day 1
  • ✓
    Access Google Drive
    drive.google.com — NEVER save client files locally
  • ☐
    Log into People HR & complete your profile
    All leave requests go through People HR — not Slack or email
  • ☐
    Enable 2-Factor Authentication on all accounts
    Gmail, Google Drive, Slack, HubSpot — mandatory from Day 1
  • ☐
    Set up your email signature
    Name, title, NEXA logo, phone, address, wearenexa.com — get logo from Ops
  • ☐
    Read this handbook end to end
    Yes, the whole thing. It'll save you a lot of "how do I…" questions later.

📅 Your First Week

Day 1
Arrive & Set Up
IT setup, access all tools, meet your team and manager.
Day 2
Learn the Brand
Study the NEXA Brand Guidelines, AM Handbook and Drive structure.
Day 3
Shadow Your Team
Sit in on stand-ups and client calls. Understand current accounts.
Day 4
Configure Your Tools
Set up Forecast for time tracking, confirm HubSpot access.
Day 5
Set Your 30-Day Goals
First 1:1 with your manager. Agree on priorities, success metrics and your learning plan.
